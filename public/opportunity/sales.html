Sales Guides

Here you will find guides on how ge become successful at selling.

Selling in person

We recommend starting with this one first to get your feet wet in sales.

In this article, we will use the Self Cleaning Hairbrush as an example product.

Step 1:

Identify your audience. For example let's take the Self Cleaning Hairbrush. Who usually uses a haribrush? People with long hair, usually girls. So this will be your target audience.

Step 2:

Mention the product. After you have identified your target audience, you can start asking them questions. For example you have a girl friend with long hair. You should start a conversation about hairbrushes for example with this line: "Wow your hair is so nice! What do you use to brush it?". This will get the conversation started. Then you can mention the self cleaning hairbrush for example like this: "Actually I work for a company who sells these cool self cleaning hairbrushes that you can clean with the press of a button".

Step 3:

Identify buyer intent. If they start asking questions about it for example "How does it work?", "How much does it cost?", you can know that they are interested. At this point you can mention that you have a coupon code they can use to get 10% OFF. You can also offer to send them the link where they can buy it.

Step 4:

Success!

Selling on TikTok

This is the best method if you want to reach a large audience and make lots of sales.

Step 1:

Create a TikTok account.

Step 2:

Search for videos about the product. For example in the case of the Self Cleaning Hairbrush, you would search for keywords like "hairbrush", "self cleaning hairbrush", etc.

Step 3:

Download the videos with a site like https://ssstik.io/en. Always download without the TikTok watermark.

Step 4:

Upload the video to your TikTok page. Make sure to put the website link in your bio and mention that they can use your coupon code to get 10% OFF! Also post it in the comments under your videos.
