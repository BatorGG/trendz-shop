Recruiting Guides

Here you will find guides on how ge become successful at recruiting.

Recruiting Acquaintances

Step 1:

Identify your target audience. The people who would be the most willing to join this alternative business opporunity are usually those who are teenagers, not old enough to get a real job, unemployed people, who would benefit from a new source of income, also students or stay at home moms.

Step 2:

Start a conversation with them. If you talk to them every day. This will be easy. If not, you will have to warm up the conversation with general topices and questions like "What's up?", "How are things going?", "What are you up to lately?", etc.

Step 3:

After warming up the conversation, mention the business opportunity. Tell them that you think they would be the perfect candidate for this opportunity. Tell them that they can create easy passive income.

Step 4:

Sign them up. Mention that generally they would have to apply and hope that they get accepted, but you have an invitation code (your coupon code) that they can use to sign up instantly.

Recruiting Strangers on Instagram

This is one of the best methods. You can do it from just your phone without knowing anyone.

Step 1:

Find a "Get rich quick" guru on Instagram. Good examples are @tailopez, @danlok, @grantcardone

Step 2:

Check the likes and comments belowe their posts. Find people who look like they would be insterested to try out a new business opportunity.

Step 3:

Send them a message inviting them to join this new business opportunity. An example message template could be:
"Hey! I checked your profile and you look like a perfect candidate to join Trendz Marketing and be part of the business opportunity they are creating. Usually you would have to apply and hope to get accepted, but I can vouch for you and give you my invitation code so you can sign up instantly. Let me know if you are interested."
If they reply, you can explain the business model to them and get them signed up.

Step 4:

After they sign up. Keep in contact with them, so you can both motivate each other and share sales tactics you can use to build your success together.

Recruiting Influencers on Instagram

This is the other best method. You can do it from just your phone without knowing anyone.

Step 1:

Find a micro-influencer on instagram. Someone with less than 100k followers but an engaged audience.

Step 2:

Message them and offer them a business opportunity. An example message template could be:
"Hey! I checked your profile and you seem like the prefect candidate to join us at Trendz Marketing and monetize your audinece. Let me know if you are interested!"

Step 3:

If they are interested, explain them the business model, tell tham that they would get a personal coupon code their audience can use to get a discount and that they would receive a commission after every sale. Also mention that usually they would have to apply and hope to get accepted, but you can vouch for them and give them your personal invitational code so they can sign up instantly.
